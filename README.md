# Wavecon-Telecom-5G-Impact-Analysis

### Project Overview

Wavecon Telecom launched 5G services in May 2022. The objective of this analysis was to evaluate the impact of the 5G launch on revenue, customer behavior, market share, and plan performance using Power BI.

### Tools Used
Power BI
Excel
Data Visualization
KPI Analysis

### Business Questions

- What was the impact of the 5G launch on revenue?
- Which KPI is underperforming after the launch?
- After the 5G launch, which plans are performing well in terms of Revenue? 
   Which plans are not performing well?
- Is there any plan affected largely by the 5G launch? Should we continue or discontinue that        plan?
- Is there any plan that is discontinued after the 5G launch? What is the reason for it?

### Key Insights

- Revenue remained relatively stable after the 5G launch.
- Active users declined while churn increased.
- New 5G plans became major revenue contributors.
- Several legacy plans experienced significant revenue decline.
- Market share decreased, indicating competitive pressure.

### Recommendations
- Strengthen customer retention initiatives.
- Optimize underperforming plans.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing 5G plans.
- Improve customer migration from legacy plans.
